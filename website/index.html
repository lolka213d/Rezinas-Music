About the app

Built for everyday listening

Dark glass UI, smart recommendations, and your library — all in one window.

Home

Fresh picks, daily mix, and recent tracks on one screen. Jump back in or discover something new without digging through menus.

  • Daily mix with a new seed every day
  • Smart playlists and quick access to favorites
  • Listening stats at a glance

My Wave

Personal radio that learns from what you play. Start the mix and let it run — skip, like, and the queue keeps flowing.

  • Endless stream tuned to your taste
  • One tap from home or the sidebar
  • Works alongside your own playlists

Playlists

Create collections, sync from Spotify, import your library, and export backups — everything stays on your PC.

  • Local playlists with drag-and-drop order
  • Spotify sync and library import
  • Export / import for backup
  • My Wave & daily mix — refreshed every day
  • Synced lyrics + full-screen overlay
  • Playlists, favorites, history, library import
  • Spotify login & playlist sync
  • Discord Rich Presence
  • 10 languages · local data · no telemetry
